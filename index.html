    Election Commission

    The election commission is mandated to foster the democratic spirit in the students’ self-governance set up - the Students’ Gymkhana. For upholding this spirit, it is necessary that the representatives of the students are elected by the process of free and fair elections.

    About Us

    The Students' Gymkhana, IIT Kanpur works through a system of democracy upheld through Gymkhana elections.We ensure a fair and smooth, end to end conduction of the Mid-Term Elections, the General Elections, HEC Elections etc.

    Our Mission

    We are an independent body which manages free and fair elections for the various Gymkhana positions and other institutional sub committees through the student participation in this democratic process of casting votes via ballot.


    Our Plan

    We would improve the efficiency of our self governed body so that elections are held in a fair and smooth manner and are well participated throughout the campus.



    Our Vision

    The Election Commission of IIT Kanpur has distinguished itself over the years and is leading with constant improvements in the service and facilities it offers to all. We promote the knowledge of sound and democratic electoral processes and promote conditions conducive to free and fair elections.
